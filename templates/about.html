DATA MANIPULATION

Illusion of freedom

Information, the currency of power

In the contemporary world, we are immersed in a sea of information. With every click, every scroll, we absorb data that influences our perceptions, beliefs, and actions. But how much of what we see, read, and hear is truly free from manipulation? How much of our supposed freedom is actually an illusion carefully constructed by those who control the flow of information?

Metal Gear Solid 2: Sons of Liberty presents us with a world where information is both a weapon and a currency of power. In this work, we witness how the invisible threads of manipulation intertwine with the fabric of society. The media, government institutions, and corporations wield control over what we know, we believe, and ultimately, how we act.

But information manipulation goes beyond mere headlines and biased news. It extends to our genes, the very core of our physical being, adopting memetic patterns that shape our behaviors in ways we barely understand. We are puppets in a dance of preconceived ideas, a product of the narrative imposed by ourselves.

The illusion of freedom is perhaps the most insidious deception of all. We believe we are free to choose, free to think, free to act. But in reality, our choices are limited by the confines of our ideas, personality and pasts experiences. We are prisoners of our own minds, of our likes and dislikes.

What can we do in a world where truth is relative and freedom is an illusion? The answer lies in awareness. We must awaken from our mental lethargy, question pre-established narratives, and seek truth beyond surface appearances, beyond our relative social realities and find it in its absolute form. Only then can we free ourselves from the yoke of manipulation and reclaim our legitimate born right.

Metal Gear Solid 2: Sons of Liberty reminds us that our human nature always seek for the ultimate. It is up to us to strife for a conscious world, to unravel the deceptions, and to seek truth in a world of illusions. Only then can we find the true freedom.
